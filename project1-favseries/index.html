The series follows Ragnar Lothbrok, a simple farmer and warrior who rises to become a legendary Viking leader through his courage and ambition. By raiding England and France, Ragnar expands Viking influence, proving himself not only as a warrior but also as an explorer and strategist. In later seasons, the focus shifts to Ragnar’s sons—Bjorn Ironside, Ivar the Boneless, Ubbe, and Hvitserk—and their great battles across England and Europe.

Characters

Ragnar Lothbrok

The main character of the series, a brave farmer who transforms into a legendary Viking warrior and king. One of the first Vikings to explore and raid England and France.

Lagertha

Ragnar’s first wife and a strong shieldmaiden (female warrior). She is one of the most iconic female characters in Viking history due to her warrior spirit and independence.

Bjorn Ironside

The son of Ragnar and Lagertha. A great Viking explorer and warrior, who eventually becomes the leader of the Vikings in later seasons.

Rollo

Ragnar’s brother, but they are in constant rivalry. He eventually leaves the Vikings and becomes the Duke of Normandy, forming an alliance with the Franks.

Ivar

Ragnar’s most ruthless and strategic son. Born crippled, but possesses an extraordinary tactical mind. One of the most feared Viking leaders to attack England.

Floki

A genius but insane shipbuilder, responsible for crafting Viking ships. He is also a deeply religious and mystical follower of Odin.

Favorite Scene

Ragnar returns victorious from England, but Jarl Haraldson feels threatened by his rise. To weaken him, Haraldson captures Rollo and brutally tortures him. This episode highlights themes of betrayal, loyalty, and power struggles as Ragnar faces a major test of his strength and leadership.
